Sound Native

English Pronunciation Coach

What is Sound Native?

Sound Native is an iOS app for serious English learners who want to understand why they sound foreign — and fix it systematically. It combines a real-time vowel analyzer, 15+ pronunciation drills, and anatomically-grounded mouth exercises.

Features

  • Real-time formant analyzer — see exactly where your vowels land
  • 15+ pronunciation drills: TH, Connected Speech, Shadowing, Intonation, Stress, Schwa, and more
  • 19 physical mouth exercises with animated diagrams
  • Accent Diagnostic to find your weakest sounds
  • Smart Queue — personalized training based on your accuracy
  • Progress tracking with streaks, trends, and 28-day calendar
  • Native language tips for Russian, Spanish, and Mandarin speakers

Privacy

All audio is processed on-device. Your voice recordings are stored locally and never transmitted. See our full privacy policy.
