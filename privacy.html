Overview

Sound Native does not collect, store, or transmit your personal information to external servers. All pronunciation analysis and progress data stays on your device.

Microphone

The app uses the microphone to analyze your pronunciation in real time. Audio is processed entirely on-device and is never recorded, uploaded, or transmitted.

Recordings

When you save a recording (shadowing, reading aloud, etc.), it is stored locally on your device only. Recordings are never sent to any server. You can delete them at any time from the Recordings Library inside the app.

Progress Data

Your practice history, accuracy scores, streaks, and exercise sessions are stored locally using standard iOS storage (UserDefaults and the app's Documents folder). This data never leaves your device.

Advertising

Free users see Google AdMob ads. AdMob may use your advertising identifier (IDFA) to show relevant ads, subject to your tracking preference set in iOS Settings. Pro subscribers see no advertisements.

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob — advertising (free tier only)
  • Apple StoreKit — in-app purchases

Children's Privacy

Sound Native is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them.

Data Deletion

Delete the app to remove all locally stored data. No data is held on external servers, so no further action is needed.

Contact

Questions? Leave feedback via the App Store or through the Support section in the app.
